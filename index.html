About Me

Dedicated to providing exceptional cancer care with compassion and expertise

I am a Consultant Medical Oncologist practicing in Bengaluru, with extensive experience in managing both solid tumors and hematological cancers. My clinical practice combines the latest in diagnostics, targeted therapies, immunotherapy, and AI-powered precision oncology to deliver truly individualized cancer care.

Cancer is not just a diagnosis for the patient — it deeply impacts the entire family. I strongly believe in addressing the emotional, psychological, and social burden that cancer brings. My approach is holistic: I not only treat the disease, but also support the mental and emotional well-being of both the-patient and their loved ones through every stage of the journey.

I also advocate for initiating best supportive care — including symptom control, nutritional support, pain management, and psychological guidance — from the very first day of diagnosis, not just at the end stages. Every patient deserves comfort, dignity, and clarity alongside curative or palliative intent.

Having trained at premier institutions and managed over a thousand patients across various cancer types and a commitment to evidence-based medicine, I aim to offer comprehensive, compassionate, and forward-thinking cancer care — where medical excellence meets human connection.
